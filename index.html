About SLESPA

A Government Registered, totally independent, not for profit, politically nonpartisan organisation

Our Heritage

SLESPA is unified with all Ex-Service and Police members of Sri Lanka who dedicated themselves during the three decades of armed conflict on land, sea and air to protect the sovereignty and save the motherland from its adversaries.

Our Mission

Consisting of ex-Sri Lankan Armed Forces and Police personnel domiciled in Australia, we provide welfare support and promote excellence amongst serving personnel of the Australian Defence Force.

Unity & Brotherhood

The association advocates a strong national feeling, friendship and unity among members, assisting the ex-service and Police community, veterans, and their families.

Community Support

We foster a strongly bonded, friendly association that recognizes the dedicated service and sacrifices made by our members, supporting both Sri Lankan and Australian communities.
