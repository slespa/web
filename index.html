About Us

Our Heritage

SLESPA is unified with all Ex-Service and Police members of Sri Lanka who dedicated themselves during the three decades of armed conflict on land, sea and air to protect the sovereignty and save the motherland from its adversaries.

Our Mission

A Government Registered, totally independent, not for profit, politically nonpartisan organisation consisting of ex-Sri Lankan Armed Forces and Police personnel domiciled in Australia.

Unity & Brotherhood

The association advocates a strong national feeling, friendship and unity among the members and is proposed to assist the ex-service and Police community, the veterans of the uniform services.

Community Support

We support ex-service and Police personnel and their families, fostering a strongly bonded, friendly association of those who served with distinction.

Our Objectives

  • Providing welfare support to the retired service and Police personnel, their families and the public
  • Promoting excellence amongst serving personnel of the Australian Defence Force and other Defence Services
  • Supporting the ex-service and Police community and veterans
  • Assisting ex-service and Police personnel and their families
  • Fostering unity and camaraderie among members through social and cultural events
